3. Voces Unidas Contra el Sexismo y la Discriminación

1. El Sexismo como Parte de un Patrón de Odio.

Las publicaciones analizadas rara vez mencionan el sexismo de forma aislada. Por lo general, se presenta como parte de una lista más amplia de discriminaciones, sugiriendo que es un componente de un sistema de odio interconectado que incluye racismo, homofobia, transfobia y otras formas de intolerancia.

  • Racism, sexism, homophobia, xenophobia, and transphobia. It’s that simple.
  • Racism, sexism, classism, respectability politics, transphobia. They all go hand in hand in hand.
  • Soo racism sexism ageism, xenophobia homophobia transphobia & misogyny. Now say that with your chest.
  • It's not just racism, it's transphobia, homophobia, sexism. It's all the ism's dammit!

2. El Sexismo No Es una Opinión, Es Intolerancia.

Un tema recurrente es la firme distinción entre una opinión válida y un prejuicio dañino. Los usuarios enfatizan que el sexismo, al igual que el racismo y la homofobia, no es un punto de vista debatible, sino una forma de intolerancia y una falla de carácter que no debe ser respetada ni aceptada en el discurso civil.

  • Racism is not a matter of opinion. Misogyny and sexism is not a matter of opinion. Xenophobia is not a matter of opinion. Trans/homophobia is not a matter of opinion.
  • Racism sexism homophobia transphobia ableism ageism religious intolerance xenophobia are not political opinions, they are bigotries. There is no agree to disagree on these topics, you're just a shitty human.
  • Racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia are not a difference of opinion.
  • Some opinions and their holders deserve hate. You can’t agree to disagree with racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia.

3. Política de Cero Tolerancia en Espacios Digitales.

Muchos usuarios declaran explícitamente una política de "cero tolerancia" hacia el sexismo y otras formas de odio en sus perfiles y comunidades. Esta postura se manifiesta en la decisión de bloquear inmediatamente a quienes expresen tales ideas, con el objetivo de crear y mantener espacios seguros y libres de hostilidad.

  • I refuse to tolerate racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, ableism, etc.
  • Things i will block people for: - homophobia - transphobia - sexism - ableism - racism - fascism - harassment life is too short to tolerate hatred. Curate a safe space.
  • Racism, homophobia, transphobia, and sexism are not allowed here and will result in instant block.
  • Just in case it wasn't abundantly clear before, transphobia, homophobia, racism, sexism, etc, is not welcome or tolerated in my space. If i ever even catch a whiff of that shit, you're gone.

4. La Interconexión de las Discriminaciones.

Varias publicaciones van más allá de la simple enumeración y exploran cómo las diferentes formas de intolerancia están conectadas. Se argumenta que el sexismo, el racismo y la homofobia a menudo provienen de las mismas estructuras de poder, como el patriarcado o la supremacía blanca, y se refuerzan mutuamente.

  • Racism , sexism , homophobia and ableism are behaviors/ideas that need to die out. They are all based on the idea that white, male, cis, hetero human beings are the default, and that all else is 'inferior'.
  • Racism sexism & homophobia are all the same energy, rooted in patriarchy & control, and i'm always confused when someone who'd be on the receiving end of oppression under one of those categories can gleefully do the same to another group.
  • Racism misogyny sexism homo and trans phobia antisemitism xenophobia ableism etc etc it’s all connected to white supremacy.
  • At the core it's an intersectional issue. Sexism links with racism links with classism links with xenophobia links with ableism links with queerphobia links with intersexism links with prejudices abound. It's all interconnected and affects each other.
