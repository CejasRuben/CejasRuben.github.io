38. 1984: De la Ficción Distópica a la Realidad Actual

1. "1984 ya no es ficción".

La percepción más extendida es que los elementos distópicos descritos por Orwell, como la vigilancia masiva, la manipulación de la verdad y el control del pensamiento, se están materializando en la sociedad actual, borrando la línea entre la novela y la vida real.

  • I don't read much but once read george orwell's "1984," which used to be fiction. Now, not so much.
  • The dystopian book “1984” is no longer fiction. It has become a prescient reality that is happening today. Wake up america, we are now being governed by a fascist authoritarian administration.
  • Scary how fiction becomes reality.
  • 1984. Yes, i know. But it is fiction that is becoming non fiction before our very eyes.
  • This is an authoritarian desire to re-write history, a la george orwell's dystopian novel, 1984 -- it's not fiction anymore.
  • I really miss living in the old timeline where 1984 seemed like an outrageous fiction.
  • We are living the dystopian fiction “1984”. The past is what they tell us it is.
