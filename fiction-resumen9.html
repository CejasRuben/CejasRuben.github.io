3. Percepciones sobre el Formato.

Los escritores definen la ficción ultracorta como un formato único que, a pesar de sus restricciones de brevedad, tiene un gran impacto. Se valora su capacidad para capturar momentos, crear tensión desde la primera frase y construir mundos con una economía de palabras precisa, considerándolo un desafío creativo gratificante.

  • Sounds intriguing! flash fiction has such a unique way of capturing moments. Can't wait to check it out!

  • Ever heard of flash fiction? they’re entire stories told in 100 words or less. I took a workshop on a whim to get back into writing, thinking it would be simple. I was wrong. It’s a challenge to build a world and a plot when every single word counts.
  • "for flash fiction, there’s a messy, resonant moment that usually comes to mind as inspiration. With such a tight word count, the tension has to be created from the first sentence, sustained through the slice of story, and end with an ambiguity that isn’t too frustrating." elana lavine.
