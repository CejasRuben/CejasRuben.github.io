28. 'The Guardian' y la Transfobia: Un Análisis Crítico

1. Críticas a la Cobertura de 'The Guardian'.

Numerosos usuarios acusan a 'The Guardian' de publicar contenido que consideran transfóbico, contribuyendo a una cultura de odio hacia las personas trans en el Reino Unido.

  • The guardian regularly publishes hateful content about trans people based on lies. It contributes directly to the culture of transphobia in the uk.
  • The thing that makes the guardian's transphobia even worse is that it is the major left leaning paper in britain. If there was going to be a newspaper counter to the rise in british transphobia, that's where it was supposed to happen.
  • The guardian launders transphobia and presents it as legitimate concerns. They are not the good guys.

2. Acusaciones de Silenciamiento y Promoción de Transfobia.

Se argumenta que 'The Guardian' tolera y fomenta la transfobia al silenciar voces trans y dar plataforma a periodistas con opiniones transfóbicas.

  • My objection is to the guardian tolerating and encouraging transphobia through silencing trans voices and doing nothing to address the transphobia by journalists such as hadley freeman or sonya sodha. It’s worse than the daily mail for transphobia.
  • The guardian is the ultimate cause of this. Your paper carried the torch for press transphobia before it was fashionable. It legitimised it as an acceptable ideology amongst “progressives”. A little self awareness and humility would be appropriate here.
  • Tbh the guardian as an institution has been such an enthusiastic driver of transphobia in the uk, since long before it was a popular position, i have to assume that almost all the staff currently there joined with full knowledge of its malevolence and are thus culpable.

3. Impacto en la Percepción Pública y la Política.

Se señala que la cobertura de 'The Guardian' normaliza la transfobia entre los progresistas y contribuye a un clima hostil hacia las personas trans en el Reino Unido.

  • The damage the guardian and observer have done with their transphobia is really bad to people who consider themselves progressive but form their opinions from papers they trust to have the correct opinion. Grim.
  • The guardian has been peddling vicious transphobia for quite some time. A very weird thing to be mourning tbh. Not to mention how it attacked the only political chance we had to no longer cater to billionaires and reduce inequality. It's a hate rag carole.
  • The way transphobia is an obsession of the british media is insane. It’s a pet project of these people. They are obsessed with being as transphobic as possible and refusing to ever share trans people’s perspectives.

4. Comparaciones con Otros Medios y Llamados a la Acción.

Se compara la cobertura de 'The Guardian' con la de otros medios, incluyendo aquellos considerados de derecha, y se insta a boicotear el periódico y a buscar fuentes de noticias alternativas.

  • The entirety of uk mainstream media from the guardian & ns to the telegraph & murdoch rags is riddled with transphobia. Any genuine alternative media is pro trans or it is not alternative media, .
  • Somehow reassuring to see the guardian and observer getting absolutely mauled for their transphobia. Dump that trashfire of a paper. I think by normalising transphobia among the soi-disant left they’ve done as much if not more harm than the right wing press.
  • Every time the guardian platforms transphobia it makes it easier to ignore when the guardian articles i read beg for money.

5. Negación y Minimización de la Transfobia.

Se critica a 'The Guardian' por no reconocer su papel en la promoción de la transfobia y por minimizar el impacto de sus acciones.

  • The guardian had the audacity to publish a piece the other day about how transphobia is targeting cis women without any self-reflection on how the guardian helped bring that about. Now they're publishing this.
  • A bit rich of the guardian to suddenly notice transphobia after peddling it for years.
  • The lack of self awareness from the guardian is staggering. They have amplified anti trans activists while ignoring trans people. But that’s different somehow (because they don’t realize or don’t care that transphobia is an in road to fascism).
