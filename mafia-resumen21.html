21. Explorando el Mundo de la Mafia en Fanfiction y Más Allá

1. La Ubicuidad de los AUs de Mafia.

El concepto de Alternate Universes (AUs) donde personajes queridos son reimaginados dentro de un contexto mafioso es un tema dominante, mostrando interpretaciones creativas a través de varios fandoms.

  • Mafia au kz is a detective struggling to pay his bills.
  • Mafia au sakuatsu where atsumu and omi are in the same group but can't stand each other.
  • Mafia au no quirk au dad afo inko is a shimura himiko toga and katsuki bakugo are (adopted) siblings.
  • Mafia au where jimin is a dancer trapped by the mafia, until one day he helps an enemy gang of six who are so charming and so kind….
  • Mafia au where robin moves to japan to teach english and steve gets legally married to her so he can go to but he can’t get a work visa so he just gets paid under the table by the karaoke bar that’s a yakuza front to stand outside and be a sorta hot white guy.

2. El Arquetipo del Jefe de la Mafia.

La figura del jefe de la mafia es central, representada con una amplia gama de personalidades, desde despiadados y temidos hasta sorprendentemente tiernos o incluso torpes.

  • Mafia boss oikawa who is dating ushijima and everyone thinks he's in a gang but it turns out he's just a big ol softie who likes gardening.
  • Mafia boss shiro as his bodyguard, but he does more than just guard his body.
  • Mafia boss komori, his right hand man suna and osamu as their hostage. That's it.
  • Sukuna is the soon to be leader of the top mafia gang in the city, he has all the money and connection in the world, everyone obeys him without question. All except his rebellious wife megumi.
  • Aqiao who is a "nice" mafia boss and pisses laoyan off because!! how can he be nice?? and sucessful?? in the mafia?!! omg i want that so muuch.

3. Dinámicas y Relaciones en el Crimen Organizado.

La intrincada red de relaciones dentro de las familias y organizaciones mafiosas, incluyendo guardaespaldas leales, rivales feroces y parejas románticas inesperadas, constituye una parte significativa de estas narrativas.

  • Dating a mafia boss had its advantages as well as its disadvantages, didn’t it?
  • Soukoku but it's an au where chuuya is the port mafia boss and dazai is his right hand man.
  • Mafia yunwoosan, wooyoung being the leader yunho his right arm and san the new delinquent doing shit on their area.
  • Carlo’s mafia group and his girlfriend’s yakuza group used to be enemies so they also used to be a love-hate relationship.
  • Mafia boss kuroo and boy toy kenma (who's actually a badass) when kenma gets kidnapped kuroo sees red.

4. La Port Mafia de Bungou Stray Dogs.

La Port Mafia de Bungou Stray Dogs es una organización particularmente prominente y detallada, frecuentemente explorada a través de los pasados de sus miembros, conflictos presentes y lealtades complejas.

  • Imagining himself as the port mafia boss is making him sick to his stomach.
  • At 18, chuuya has fallen in love with dazai. He should hate dazai for leaving him behind when he deflects the mafia.
  • Dazai knows all too well about chuuya's lifestyle even if he doesn't agree, but chuuya's so deeply entrenched in the heart of the mafia that all he can do is stand by and watch from the sidelines helplessly.
  • Port mafia yosano in particular haunts me like a gothic spectre.
  • Dazai who won't step foot in the mafia headquarters unless he came home and chuuya wasn't there.
