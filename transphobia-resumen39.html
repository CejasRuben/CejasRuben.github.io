39. Transfobia: Un Análisis de Clase y Poder

1. Transfobia como Herramienta de División de Clase.

Muchos argumentan que la transfobia es utilizada por las élites para dividir a la clase trabajadora y distraerla de problemas económicos fundamentales.

  • Transphobia is another way of introducing divide and conquer amongst the working class, distracting from the real reasons for social disfunction: poverty and a widening wealth gap.
  • Transphobia is designed to flood the zone with false concerns so voters minds are occupied with that rather than how empty their wallets are.
  • Transphobia plays an important function for the ruling class - divide and rule.

2. La Transfobia y el Capitalismo.

Se sugiere que el capitalismo no solo permite, sino que también refuerza la transfobia, ya que se beneficia de la división y la explotación.

  • Transphobia is a byproduct of white supremacy, and capitalism reinforces it.
  • I wish more people were class conscious, and weren't brainwashed by capitalism and white supremacy, cos they'd know that white supremacy and capitalism work hand-in-hand, and that transphobia is white supremacist ideology and capitalism props it up.
  • Racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, all of these are intentionally propagated and fueled by capitalism to reinforce a class system, to have an exploitable class. Because it makes someone money.

3. La Transfobia como Preocupación de la Élite.

Algunos argumentan que la transfobia es principalmente una preocupación de las clases altas, quienes tienen el tiempo y los recursos para invertir en ella.

  • Transphobia is a luxury belief in that the only people seriously invested in it are wealthy white people.
  • Transphobia is really a bourgeois fixation. People with bills to pay simply don’t have time to be insane about it.
  • Once again: transphobia is an obsession of the elites. This is a war waged by the gentry upon the rest of us.

4. Interseccionalidad: Clase, Raza y Transfobia.

Se reconoce la importancia de la interseccionalidad, señalando que la transfobia interactúa con otros sistemas de opresión como el racismo y el sexismo.

  • “it’s all just a class war” says the person desperately trying to avoid discussions of racism, sexism, homophobia and transphobia in their political discourse. Things can be multifaceted, that’s why intersectionality exists.".
  • Almost like transphobia and racism are both connected by global racial capitalism oh wait.
  • It’s class, race and several other prejudices that all feed into transphobia (eg non-white women athletes are much more likely to be “transvestigated”, their attitude to people with autism is atrocious etc etc ).

5. Transfobia y la Lucha Socialista.

Existe un debate sobre si el socialismo es una solución para la transfobia, con algunos argumentando que es esencial para la liberación trans y otros cuestionando la historia de los movimientos socialistas en relación con los derechos LGBTQ+.

  • And socialism is how you fight/stop it. Not more liberalism or liberal identity reductionism.
  • Enstating socialism or whatever would not end transphobia. But you also can't end transphobia without first ending capitalism.
  • What really gets me is when trans and other queer people insist that ending capitalism will end transphobia, as if we’ve done great under communism.
