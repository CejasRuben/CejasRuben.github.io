26. Transfobia en el Ámbito Laboral: Experiencias y Consecuencias

1. Impacto de la Transfobia en el Empleo.

La transfobia en el ámbito laboral se manifiesta en despidos injustificados, dificultades para encontrar empleo y un ambiente de trabajo hostil.

  • I've been fired twice for being trans. They don't say that, of course, but i'm sure that's why. The transphobia is real, and i don't think it'll improve in our society in the coming years.
  • Transphobia is so out of control you can be bullied, then fired for being bullied when you're not even trans.
  • It's definitely a little striking just how good transphobia is at destroying careers, honestly. Not necessarily even because of the social opprobium: it just seems to have a way of consuming a person's entire self.

2. Formas Sutiles y Abiertas de Transfobia.

La transfobia se presenta de manera sutil, como microagresiones y comentarios casuales, hasta formas más directas como el acoso y la discriminación explícita.

  • Love being told the transphobia is just in my head.
  • My coworkers sometimes drop random transphobia in front of me. They don't know i'm trans. I can't say a word bc they obviously hate me without knowing it. I can't risk my job and safety by saying something. So i have to just tolerate bigotry to my face.
  • Lots of transphobia at the work meeting over my coworker’s pronouns. Amongst plenty of other morally objectionable shit, i can’t do this anymore. I’m so tired man.

3. Estrés y Salud Mental Afectados por la Transfobia.

El constante enfrentamiento a la transfobia genera estrés, ansiedad y un deterioro general de la salud mental en las personas trans.

  • I feel like i'm about two disasters away from doing myself in. Transphobia can go to fucking hell!
  • There are some days that the transphobia really gets to you. Today is that day. Cranky and hurt and angry and sad.
  • I'm crashing tf out. Typed a whole big thing but deleted it. I'm just tired of transphobia being normalized and even pushed. I'm always scared and it's always justified. ,-,.

4. Búsqueda de Soluciones y Apoyo.

Ante la transfobia, se buscan soluciones como el apoyo de la gerencia, la búsqueda de nuevos empleos y la denuncia de incidentes, aunque con temor a represalias.

  • Oh, i'm also dealing with some straight up, absolutely blatant asshole transphobia at work. Management's in my corner which is nice, but it's stressful & tiring, and i'd be really happy if it were just not a thing that was going on.
  • Actually, i've also been telling my supervisors and bosses explicitly im looking for work because growing transphobia has made living here dangerous. Let them know the cause of hate.
  • I've been outspoken about this organization's transphobia for years, as many of us have been (to our professional detriment, it's often felt). Nothing about this is logical or fun. So annoyed i've managed to yet again get this irate at the times.
