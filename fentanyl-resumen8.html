8. El fentanilo es traficado mayormente por ciudadanos estadounidenses

1. Ciudadanos estadounidenses como principales traficantes.

Un tema central y recurrente en los datos es que la gran mayoría de las personas condenadas por tráfico de fentanilo son ciudadanos estadounidenses. Las cifras citadas consistentemente oscilan entre el 80% y más del 96%, contradiciendo directamente la idea de que los migrantes o solicitantes de asilo son los principales responsables.

  • The majority of fentanyl brought across the border from mexico is by american citizens.
  • 86 percent of of those sentenced for fentanyl trafficking are us citizens. Us citizens are bringing fentanyl across our borders.
  • The vast majority--89%--of fentanyl entering the country is trafficked by u.s. Citizens.
  • In 2021, u.s. Citizens were 86.3 percent of convicted fentanyl drug traffickers—ten times greater than convictions of illegal immigrants for the same offense.
  • A more accurate summary is that fentanyl is overwhelmingly smuggled by u.s. Citizens, almost entirely for u.s. Citizen consumers.
  • Nearly 99% of all fentanyl is smuggled into america by u.s. Citizens at legal border checkpoints.
  • According to the u.s. Sentencing commission, 86.4% of people sentenced for fentanyl trafficking crimes in a 12-month period ending september 2023 were american citizens.

2. Puertos de entrada legales como ruta principal.

Los datos indican que el fentanilo no ingresa principalmente a través de rutas de migración irregular en zonas remotas. En cambio, la abrumadora mayoría es incautada en puertos de entrada legales, como cruces fronterizos, aeropuertos y puertos marítimos, oculto en vehículos conducidos por ciudadanos autorizados a cruzar.

  • Over 90% of the fentanyl seized at the mexico border is smuggled in by us citizens at points of entry.
  • For the record, over 96% of fentanyl is smuggled in by americans in vehicles through manned checkpoints from mexico. None with immigrants.
  • Most fentanyl is smuggled into the us through legal ports of entry by americans.
  • 90% of fentanyl comes in at border crossings in vehicles driven by us citizens.
  • The vast majority of fentanyl comes through legal ports of entry. And the people bringing it into the country are native born americans.
  • Report: most seizures of fentanyl occur at u.s. Points of entry, not via migrants. In reality, u.s. Citizens are the primary smugglers.
  • The majority of fentanyl enters through official land ports of entry, hidden in vehicles, commercial trucks, and passenger luggage.

3. Desmintiendo la narrativa sobre la inmigración.

Las publicaciones contrastan explícitamente los hechos con la narrativa política que culpa a los inmigrantes indocumentados. Se enfatiza que el tráfico de fentanilo no es un problema de inmigración, ya que los migrantes rara vez son encontrados con la droga, y los traficantes son abrumadoramente ciudadanos que enfrentan menor escrutinio en la frontera.

  • Most of the fentanyl that comes into the u.s. From mexico is brought by u.s. Citizens, not migrants.
  • 90% of fentanyl is smuggled by us citizens for u.s. Citizens at legal border checkpoints. It’s not an immigration-related issue.
  • The right never lets facts get in the way of a good story. Most fentanyl is brought into this country thru legal ports of entry by us citizens. But, but, but, migrants.
  • The idea that hordes of illegal immigrants are crossing the desert with bags of fentanyl on their backs is a lie.
  • Fentanyl is smuggled for u.s. Citizens by u.s. Citizens, not asylum seekers.
  • Just 0.02 percent of the people arrested by border patrol for crossing illegally possessed any fentanyl whatsoever.
  • The vast majority of fentanyl is trafficked into the u.s. By u.s. Citizens, not asylum seekers or undocumented immigrants.
