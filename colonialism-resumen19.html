1. Definiciones y Perspectivas del Colonialismo.

El concepto de colonialismo es ampliamente debatido, abarcando desde la explotación territorial y económica hasta la imposición cultural. Se discute si el colonialismo se limita a la era europea moderna o si es un fenómeno recurrente a lo largo de la historia.

  • Colonialism is primarily when a foreign power exploits either a military or trade advantage to establish a dominant bloc in your society that is given political power in exchange for tribute in taxes and trade policy. They then run the country.
  • Colonialism refers to a historical practice where one nation establishes control over another territory, often through conquest, settlement, or exploitation. This domination typically involves the subjugation of local populations, imposition of foreign governance, and extraction of resources.
  • Colonialism is a relationship of power justified through ethnonationalist claims, not an argument about who was where first.
