5. La Existencia Trans como Amenaza al Patriarcado

1. Las Mujeres Trans como Amenaza al Orden Patriarcal.

Esta sección explora la idea recurrente de que las mujeres trans representan una amenaza fundamental para el patriarcado. Al rechazar la masculinidad —considerada superior en este sistema— y abrazar la feminidad, desafían la jerarquía de género establecida, lo que genera una fuerte disonancia cognitiva y hostilidad por parte de quienes defienden dicho orden.

  • We are one of the biggest threats to the patriarchy. The fact that trans women reject manhood while embracing womanhood, causes extreme cognitive dissonance to people whose entire world view is predicated on the idea of men being superior. Nothing pisses them off more than seeing us thrive.
  • Trans women are a direct threat to the foundation of patriarchy. Because a trans woman says: "i see male privilege. I was handed it. And i still chose truth. Chose authenticity. Chose to be a woman." and that breaks their brains.
  • It's pretty clear to me why trans women are such a perennial target for the patriarchy. The very fact that a man would choose to become a woman flies in the face of the old order where men are supposed to be inherently superior. By virtue of existing, a trans woman kicks misoginy where it hurts.
  • Patriarchy hates trans women because it loathes the idea that being a woman is at all desirable.
  • The patriarchy is more opposed to trans women than anything else because the idea that a "man" would "choose" to be a woman dismantles the patriarchal idea that men are superior to women.

2. El Complejo Rol de los Hombres Trans en el Patriarcado.

Los posts revelan una discusión compleja y a menudo contradictoria sobre la posición de los hombres trans dentro de la estructura patriarcal. Mientras que algunos argumentan que también son oprimidos y vistos como "mujeres fallidas" por el sistema, otros sostienen que pueden participar o incluso beneficiarse de las estructuras patriarcales, a diferencia de las mujeres trans.

  • I’ve always wondered how trans men relate to patriarchy. To my understanding, the vast majority reject it on principle bc it’s vile shit, but there’s probably a lot more going on that i don’t see.
  • The want to completely decouple trans men as men from patriarchy, that they cannot ever possibly benefit from, enforce, or participate in it is just anti-reality. Even cis women can participate in, enforce, and even 'benefit' from patriarchy!
  • I suspect trans men fit the narrative of patriarchy better. To the patriarchal mind a woman who want to be a man to be the superior gender makes sense, but a man who would surrender their superior gender is a threatening aberration.
  • This smacks of “trans men are failed women who are trying to become the patriarchy”, it’s very bad.
  • I don't know why this constantly needs to be explained to people, but trans men and trans mascs are not seen as men in a transphobic society and do not inherit the benefits of the patriarchy.

3. El Patriarcado como Raíz de la Transfobia.

Un argumento central en el documento es que el patriarcado es la fuente última de toda transfobia. Se postula que la existencia de personas trans subvierte los roles de género rígidos en los que se basa el sistema patriarcal, castigando a quienes no se ajustan a las normas prescritas y convirtiéndolos en un objetivo para mantener el control social.

  • The patriarchy is the source of all transphobia. It punishes all trans people for breaking its prescribed roles about gender and sex.
  • Trans people are a massive disruptance to patriarchy. They’re seen as “people giving up their manhood to become weak” or “weak people gaining manhood” and misogynists hate that, they don’t want people changing their status.
  • If you want to fight transphobia, you have to address patriarchy.
  • Trans people threaten the whole foundation of the patriarchy. Of course they must be eliminated!
  • Trans people transitioning is the biggest threat to patriarchy. Completely subverting the trope of natural order, compliance, assimilation, hierarchy that patriarchy relies on, and turning it on its head.

Estos posts argumentan que las mujeres trans son hombres que buscan invadir espacios femeninos y mantener el privilegio masculino bajo un disfraz diferente.

  • It is patriarchy but in a trojan horse. Trans women are not women, that's all there is to it.

  • I have learned that the patriarchy is not above putting on a dress or changing their pronouns in order to abuse women. Not everyone who is trans has to be consciously acting as an agent of the patriarchy in order for gender ideology to serve patriarchal goals.
  • Trans women (biological males) are erasing biological women and taking their rights away. Stop this nonsense. It's patriarchy disguised in dresses.
