20. Transfobia y Homofobia: Un Odio Entrelazado

1. La Conexión entre Transfobia y Homofobia.

Numerosos comentarios describen la transfobia como una forma reciclada o reenvasada de la homofobia. Se argumenta que ambas surgen de las mismas raíces de intolerancia, misoginia y el rechazo a quienes desafían las normas de género tradicionales.

  • Transphobia recycles so much homophobia because the backbone of transphobia is homophobia.
  • I’ve faced both homophobia & transphobia & they are both out of the same hateful playbook. The same people who hate lgb people hate the t.
  • Transphobia is just homophobia. All of the homophobic bigots who’ve been biting their tongues for years are secretly thrilled that they found a currently “acceptable” way to voice their hatred.
  • Transphobia is just spicy homophobia and homophobia is just spicy misogyny. They're all wriggling arms of the same dipshit octopus.

2. Transfobia Dentro de la Comunidad Queer.

Una temática recurrente es la decepción y el dolor causados por la transfobia presente en espacios LGBTQ+. Muchos testimonios señalan directamente a hombres cisgénero gais como una fuente significativa de esta hostilidad interna.

  • Unfortunately, there is a lot of transphobia within the queer community. Much like there's a lot of misogyny among gay men that nobody wants to talk about.
  • To be fair all of the transphobia i've experienced so far has been from gay men.
  • It is so upsetting to know that transphobia exists even in the gay community. The t belongs very firmly in the lgbtqia!
  • The transphobia among gay men is wild… like, sir, you have rights because of a latin and black trans woman. Lesbians and trans women took care of you during the aids epidemic. Transphobia from gay men is bullshit.

3. Más Allá del Miedo: Definiendo la Intolerancia.

Algunos usuarios cuestionan la idoneidad de los términos "fobia", argumentando que no se trata de un miedo irracional, sino de simple intolerancia, odio y prejuicio. Se sugiere que estas actitudes están más relacionadas con la inseguridad y la masculinidad tóxica.

  • It's not transphobia or homophobia: those terms mean fear of trans or gay folks. Just straight up bigotry and hatred.
  • It's not really "transphobia" or "homophobia", phobia describes something you're afraid of. They're not afraid, they're simply assholes.
  • Homophobia transphobia are all part of prejudice bigot hate with toxic masculinity.
  • So let's call homophobia and transphobia what they actually are: people being shitty to other people for no real reason other than they decided they could get away with it.

4. Diferencias y Matices entre Ambas Opresiones.

A pesar de sus vínculos, varios comentarios subrayan que la transfobia y la homofobia son fenómenos distintos con mecanismos y consecuencias diferentes. Se advierte contra la simplificación de ver una como un mero subproducto de la otra, ya que cada una tiene sus propias particularidades.

  • Transphobia functions very differently than homophobia. Being out isn’t necessarily desirable or possible.
  • Your approach here is so unnecessarily divisive. Homophobia exists. Transphobia exists. They share some common roots. They are not the same thing. What is vital is that we work together to oppose them.
  • Also people use this fact to try to claim that transphobia is just homophobia rebranded and that is a really reductive and false understanding of what transphobia is.
  • I think that while a lot of homophobia and transphobia is rooted in the same "failure to meet gendered behaviour norms", that doesn't make them the same thing. I have faced homophobia but i have never faced transphobia.
