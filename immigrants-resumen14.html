14. Solidaridad en Acción: Comunidades se Unen para Apoyar a Inmigrantes

1. Llamados a la Acción y Protesta Comunitaria.

El documento refleja una movilización constante a través de manifestaciones, vigilias y paros comunitarios. Estas acciones buscan demostrar apoyo a los inmigrantes, mantener la atención pública sobre las acciones de ICE y resistir las políticas consideradas inhumanas, reafirmando el poder y la unidad de la comunidad.

  • Concerned about administration detaining our neighbors? demonstrate that our community cares: join rights rally every sun, 10:30am-12pm at union & main. Bring signs that support immigrants & immigration.
  • Immigrants are welcome here! in light of the attacks against our communities, immigrants & allies are participating in a 24 community stoppage to reclaim our power and fight back against the trump terror! the ice raids must end, because our families belong together!
  • Community action: •escucha mi voz is organizing a “day of action” rally at the ice office on sept. 2, 7:30 a.m. •goal: show support for immigrants and keep public attention on ice’s actions.
  • If you are in nyc and want to show your support to immigrants, please consider joining a silent vigil against their abduction and deportation. Protesters have anti-ice signs. Every thursday, 12-1 pm 26 federal plaza (worth st.), nyc.

2. Apoyo de Negocios Locales y Solidaridad Visible.

Una estrategia clave destacada es la colaboración con empresas locales. Se anima a los ciudadanos a pedir a los dueños de negocios que muestren carteles de apoyo a los inmigrantes y de oposición a ICE. Esta táctica no solo crea espacios seguros, sino que también envía un mensaje claro de que la comunidad empresarial respalda a sus vecinos inmigrantes.

  • “we can and must stand up for immigrants and show that they are essential and welcomed members of our communities. … (we want to) ask the business owners or managers to post signs that declare their support for their immigrant neighbors and opposition to ice.”.
  • One of the things they are asking us to do is to talk to people at local businesses that we frequent and ask them to post signs of support of immigrants and mark employee spaces as private to prevent ice from entering.
  • Join this ongoing team project to visit businesses in your neighborhood and ask them to step up to protect immigrants. We’re headed out (in groups at first) to ask business owners and managers to post signs that declare their support for their immigrant neighbors and opposition to ice.
  • Signs of solidarity is a new initiative to stand against fascist ice raids by placing signs to protect and support immigrants in local businesses. We’re asking indivisibles to canvass local businesses in defiance of ice.

3. Defensa de Derechos y Redes de Apoyo Mutuo.

Se enfatiza la importancia de la organización comunitaria para la protección directa. Esto incluye la creación de redes de respuesta rápida para documentar redadas, la formación de observadores legales para monitorear las acciones de ICE y la organización de acompañamientos a citas judiciales o escolares para quienes temen salir de sus hogares.

  • Look up your closest "indivisible" group. Our local group makes relay calls when we hear about an ice raid and support immigrants in knowing their rights:
  • Community saves community! if you see ice in your neighborhood, document it and call us to activate our rapid response network 88-624-4752. We know immigrants belong & we will continue to show up to defend our communities and keep families united!
  • “our solidarity shows up in many ways. We accompany immigrants to court hearings. We help families whose breadwinners have been detained. We take children to school when parents are too afraid to leave home. And, we raise funds to increase access to legal representation.”.
  • Get training and become an ice legal observer today. Help the immigrants, help each other. Know your rights.

4. Afirmación de Pertenencia y Contribución Inmigrante.

El mensaje central es que los inmigrantes son una parte esencial e integral de la comunidad. Se les describe no como extraños, sino como vecinos, amigos, compañeros de trabajo y familiares que fortalecen el tejido social y económico del país. Esta narrativa busca contrarrestar la criminalización y reafirmar su valor y dignidad.

  • "many great organizations are working hard to defend the rights, dignity & wellbeing of immigrants, refugees & the undocumented. People who, like many of us or our families, come to this country & make it a better, stronger place as they seek a better life.".
  • Immigrants are vital contributors to local communities – neighbors, friends, coworkers, caretakers and more. Take action today:
  • Immigrants are our neighbors, they are the backbone of this city, and they are here to stay.
  • Immigrants are the foundation of our communities, building our nation with strength, resilience, and hope.
