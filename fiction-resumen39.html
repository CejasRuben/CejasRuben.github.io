39. Ficción por Suscripción: Autores Usan Substack y Patreon

1. Promoción Directa: Substack y Patreon como Canales Principales.

Los autores utilizan activamente plataformas de suscripción como Substack y Patreon para promocionar su trabajo. Ofrecen una mezcla de contenido gratuito y de pago, que incluye ficción, ensayos personales y reseñas, buscando aumentar su base de suscriptores y construir una comunidad directa con sus lectores.

  • Hi new followers! shameless plug for my substack, which is free to all, no paid subscription. I write book reviews, personal essays, and wips of my fiction; genres include gothic, erotica and dark fiction.
  • A good time to go over to my patreon and subscribe for mere pennies! i've posted a new piece of fiction there today - (and there's an archive all kinds of otherwise unavailable things to access.).
  • So, i'm really hoping to boost my paid subscribers at my substack newsletter this month. I'm a freelance critic and fiction writer, and so there's a lot of both commentary/analysis and creative stuff there, everything from book and movie and tv reviews to short stories (mostly romance and fantasy).
  • New followers may also want to know about my patreon, where i write on more pop culture and where i'm debuting my fiction, including a forthcoming novel. Free and paid tiers, tell your friends.

2. Oferta de Contenido: Ficción Serializada, Relatos Cortos y Más.

La oferta de contenido es variada, destacando la ficción serializada, los relatos cortos mensuales y el acceso a archivos exclusivos. Los géneros abarcan desde la fantasía y la ciencia ficción hasta el terror, el erotismo y la ficción gótica, atrayendo a diferentes nichos de lectores.

  • From my patreon. Subscriptions as cheap as 1 a month. Fiction, serialized novels.
  • If you're looking for short stories of fantasy, fiction, and sci-fi, you should check out my patreon where i post a short story every month!
  • Fresh serial fiction each week, sneak peeks and snippets each month, a private discord, and more. Come help me build new worlds!
  • I write, mostly about horror movies, w/ occasional fiction etc. Book on preorder, check bio.
  • I primarily write fantasy when it comes to stories though my posts on substack aren't that lol. Loser is more a character study modern fiction and morning glow is speculative fiction.

3. Modelos de Acceso: Contenido Gratuito y Niveles de Pago.

Los creadores emplean un modelo mixto, ofreciendo gran parte de su contenido de forma gratuita para atraer a nuevos seguidores, mientras que reservan material exclusivo, como capítulos adelantados, versiones en audio o acceso a comunidades privadas, para los suscriptores de pago.

  • Hi-hi. I've made a big decision to demonetize my creative writing from here on out: fiction, nonfiction, and poetry included. So i'm on substack now! please consider subscribing (for free!!)!
  • New short fiction every week or so. And paid subscribers get access to audio versions of each post.
  • Science fiction short story now available on my substack ps it’s free today only! it will be paywalled tomorrow. Don’t miss out!
  • Free to read for all members you can join my page for free and get access to all my newsletter-style announcement posts! (and also my flos magicae bonus epilogues ) paid members get access to exclusive fiction on top of that.

4. Construcción de Comunidad y Búsqueda de Interacción.

Más allá de la publicación, los escritores buscan activamente construir una comunidad. Utilizan sus plataformas para interactuar con los lectores, solicitar retroalimentación y ofrecer espacios exclusivos como servidores de Discord, fomentando un sentido de pertenencia y apoyo mutuo.

  • I'm trying to expand my to boost my community, and offering free writing, patreon memberships, & more!
  • Publishing fiction on substack is a strange mix of hope, hustle, and hitting “send” into the void. But that void spoke back. 19 readers deep. That’s 19 people who chose my story over scrolling.
  • A monthly or weekly fiction fix, weekly serial chapters, votes on upcoming serials or short stories, plus access to my private discord server.
  • I am practicing writing fiction on my substack linked here. I would be happy for anyone to follow me (you can follow and read for free despite the 5 button). I look forward to peoples feedback.
