8. Bloqueos y Listas: La Lucha contra la Transfobia Online

1. Listas de Bloqueo como Herramienta de Defensa.

Los usuarios emplean activamente listas de bloqueo (blocklists) como un mecanismo proactivo para filtrar contenido de odio y curar sus líneas de tiempo. Estas listas, a menudo colaborativas, permiten a los usuarios bloquear masivamente cuentas identificadas como transfóbicas, creando así espacios digitales más seguros y libres de acoso.

  • Finally here's a transphobia blocklist. Even if you're not trans or a trans ally, these folk are really irritating.
  • Wow that guy reskeets a lot of transphobia. I'm just using him as a source for people to block before i block him.
  • The transphobia in this thread is pretty gross, but i suppose it's a useful list of people to block.
  • I've blocked a few transphobes already. I'm even subscribed to a blocklist dedicated to erasing transphobia. So yeah. Transphobia has absolutely no place on my feed.
  • Or just subscribe to some anti-transphobia block lists. They'll save you so much time.

2. Controversia y Desconfianza en las Listas.

A pesar de su utilidad, las listas de bloqueo generan controversia. Muchos usuarios reportan haber sido añadidos injustamente a listas de "transfobia" por desacuerdos no relacionados o por actores maliciosos. Esto ha llevado a una creciente desconfianza, ya que las listas pueden ser instrumentalizadas para silenciar a personas inocentes, incluyendo a miembros de la propia comunidad trans.

  • Yeah, this is why i don't subscribe to transphobia blocklists. Always a chance of an innocent person getting caught up in them.
  • This one added me to their transphobia block list, even though i have never been transphobic in my life. It's just one of many lists whose owners make up reasons to add people.
  • There are a number of blocklists that claim to be combating transphobia which are full of trans people who have voiced nothing of the sort. I'm on a few.
  • For what it's worth, there are numerous "transphobia" blocklists on this website which are run by absolute clowns, and work to silence and isolate trans women following fights within the community over trolling between different cliques.
  • I got thrown on 3 transphobia block list simply for disagreeing with someone who just happened to be trans about something that had nothing at all to do with their trans identity.

3. Etiquetas y Advertencias como Sistema de Moderación.

Además de los bloqueos directos, los usuarios utilizan servicios de etiquetado (labelers) que marcan perfiles con advertencias de contenido, como "transfobia" o "transmisoginia". Este sistema ofrece un enfoque más matizado, permitiendo a los usuarios estar informados sobre el historial de una cuenta y decidir por sí mismos si interactuar, silenciar o bloquear, en lugar de aplicar un bloqueo automático.

  • This person has transphobia mute and block lists you can subscribe to. You can have content hidden or warnings, etc.
  • Worth noting that account is flagged as "transphobia" by both transphobia labelers i'm subscribed to. So likely an account worth a heads up about.
  • Um, i'm seeing a transphobia label on your profile as well. Is that true? i don't block that list so i can give people a chance to know/dispute.
  • Coincidently, your account has been labeled as transphobia (general) by one of the labeling services i am trying out.
  • Today i learned about an account labeler that marks accounts for transphobia. That way you dont accidentally follow a phobe.

4. La Acción Individual: Bloquear y Reportar.

Más allá de las herramientas automatizadas, la acción individual sigue siendo una piedra angular en la lucha contra el odio. Los usuarios adoptan una política de "tolerancia cero", bloqueando y reportando inmediatamente cualquier cuenta que muestre comportamiento transfóbico. Esta respuesta directa y personal es vista como una forma esencial de mantener la integridad de sus espacios y de la plataforma en general.

  • Ah, experienced my first bit of transphobia on here. Blocked & reported.
  • If i see you engaging in transphobia, that's an instant block. This includes deadnaming even the shittiest trans people.
  • Just for the record, i have a zero-tolerance policy towards transphobia and if i see it in my mentions it's an instant block.
  • I hate having to block people, but i will absolutely block transphobia. This non-binary femme is so tired of this shit, y'all. Sheesh.
  • Transphobia will get you blocked fast. It’s especially disgusting coming from people within the lgbtq community.
