30. Transfobia en Videojuegos: Percepciones y Controversias Online

1. Acusaciones de Transfobia en Juegos Específicos.

Usuarios expresan preocupación y acusan a ciertos juegos y desarrolladores de transfobia, citando contenido problemático, financiación de figuras transfóbicas y la creación de comunidades tóxicas.

  • "the game supports transphobia by funding the author, who is transphobic and actively puts her money into making life harder for trans people.".
  • "play game for fun community and cute art style look inside transphobia homophobia racism what the fuck?".
  • "and if you buy this game know that you're directly supporting transphobia and should be considered an enemy.".

2. Defensas y Minimización de la Transfobia.

Algunos jugadores defienden los juegos acusados de transfobia, argumentando que las acusaciones son exageradas, que los elementos problemáticos son malinterpretaciones o que la intención no era dañina.

  • "going insane seeing queer people defending a game with transphobia because it was due to a bad translation. The product is still transphobic, it doesn’t matter how it happened".
  • "korone is just playing a video game. If you think that's magically transphobia, you have a few screws loose. Educate yourself.".
  • "my understanding is that the guy is actually just an asshole but the transphobia specifically was language barrier issues.".

3. Impacto en Jugadores Trans y la Comunidad LGBTQ+.

La transfobia en los videojuegos genera sentimientos de inseguridad, exclusión y enojo en jugadores trans y miembros de la comunidad LGBTQ+, afectando su disfrute y participación en los espacios de juego.

  • "transphobia in my fcking fav game makes me extra angry, especially when is a super lgbtfriendly game. I fucking hate players".
  • "i'm just tired of transphobia being cloaked as criticising 'poor writing' or 'bad wordlbuilding'. No one has anything against queer representation but god forbid that representation is visible in the game".
  • "our queer gaming group plays codenames a lot. But honestly, fuck you if you're going to embrace transphobia".

4. Llamados a la Acción y Responsabilidad.

Usuarios exigen a desarrolladores, streamers y la comunidad en general que tomen medidas contra la transfobia, promuevan la inclusión y creen espacios seguros para jugadores trans.

  • "there are so, so many good games out there that don’t support transphobia. It’s such an easy choice to make".
  • "transphobia has no place in gaming. Not at any company i work at, not in any company i’ve *worked* with. I will not negotiate over tolerance for human existence and dignity.".
  • "it's always good to remind people that not playing a game with transphobia in takes zero effort. Just. Don't do it. It's that easy.".
