36. Espacios Seguros Online: Tolerancia Cero a la Transfobia

1. Declaraciones de Intolerancia.

Usuarios expresan explícitamente su rechazo a la transphobia y otras formas de odio en sus perfiles y comunidades online.

  • "this page is zero tolerance for transphobia, homophobia, racism, fascism, sexual harassment/assault, misogyny or ableism. I'm a queer disabled babe not afraid to use the block button".
  • "homophobia and transphobia will not be tolerated on my page. You will automatically be called out and blocked.".
  • "i have zero tolerance for homophobia or transphobia, please miss me with that bullshit. This is a trans friendly/lgbtq friendly page.".

2. Creación de Espacios Seguros.

Se enfatiza la importancia de crear y mantener espacios online donde las personas LGBTQ+ y otras minorías se sientan seguras y respetadas.

  • "i just want everyone to know: my page is a safe space. I do not tolerate bigotry like sexism, racism, homophobia, transphobia, xenophobia, etcetera. Please, do not come near me if you want to be a monster.".
  • "this will always be a lgbtq safe space.".
  • "our account will always be a safe space for all gender identities, expressions, and sexual orientations!".

3. Consecuencias para la Transgresión.

Se advierte sobre las consecuencias de expresar opiniones o comportamientos transphobic, incluyendo el bloqueo y el reporte de cuentas.

  • "if i see any kind of homophobia transphobia enbyphobia or any kind of bigtory on my page i will block your ass and then report you without fail.".
  • "if you have a transphobia, get off my page. I will not tolerate that bullshit. I will drag you through the mud.".
  • "reminder: transphobia is absolutely not tolerated at all in my space. You will be banned and blocked. Consider this your only warning".
