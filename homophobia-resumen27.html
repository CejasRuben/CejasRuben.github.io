27. Homofobia: Un Pilar Central en la Ideología Fascista

1. La Homofobia como Componente Esencial del Fascismo.

Los textos argumentan que la homofobia, junto con otras formas de intolerancia como el racismo, la misoginia y la transfobia, no es un subproducto del fascismo, sino uno de sus pilares ideológicos. Se presenta como una característica definitoria e inseparable de este movimiento.

  • Fascism always involves bigotry, with racism, misogyny, transphobia/homophobia and religious demonisation at its core.
  • Racism, homophobia, transphobia, misogyny, and ableism aren't smokescreens to distract from fascism. They are the fascism.
  • The misogyny - and for that matter the racism the homophobia the xenophobia - are not incidental or adjacent to fascism. They are at its core.
  • Its important to recognise that racism, misogyny and homophobia form the basis of the far-right's claim to power. Fight bigotry and you're delegitimising facism.

2. La Homofobia como Arma Estratégica.

Se describe la homofobia como una herramienta deliberadamente utilizada por los movimientos fascistas. Su propósito es generar división, crear chivos expiatorios, movilizar a sus bases y consolidar el poder a través de la deshumanización de grupos minoritarios.

  • You need to recognize that transphobia and homophobia are weapons of fascism and not issues to be swept to the side.
  • Fascism loves for its weapons to be used. It thrives on the divisiveness of those weapons. It feeds and grows the more they're used. Transphobia, sexism, racism, homophobia, and xenophobia are powerful.
  • Now seems like a good time to to remind everyone that while fascist authoritarian movements are built on ‘blood and soil’ nationalism, xenophobia, and antisemitism, they are fueled by weaponization of misogyny and homophobia, institutionalization of patriarchy, and rigid enforcement of gender norms.
  • It's surprising to me how many people can't understand where the transphobia and homophobia are coming from when it's always been one of the main tenets of fascism. In short, it's about control and out-breeding the undesirables.

3. El Rechazo a la Homofobia en la Lucha Antifascista.

Una idea recurrente es que la lucha contra el fascismo es incompatible con el uso de la homofobia o cualquier otra forma de intolerancia, incluso cuando se dirige contra los propios fascistas. Se sostiene que adoptar sus tácticas es contraproducente y socava los principios del antifascismo.

  • You cannot fight fascism by doing ableism or homophobia. Doing that is just doing the fascists work for them under the guise of "resistance".
  • You can't be antifa(scist) and a bigot. This includes finding misogyny, transphobia, homophobia and racism et. Al. Funny. You can't be against the fascists while sharing their core values.
  • Quick reminder. If you use ableism, fat shaming, transphobia, racism, homophobia, etc, to own a fascist, you're no different to said fascist. Do better.
  • Homophobia doesn't help get rid of fascists. Please try again and do better.
