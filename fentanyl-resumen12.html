12. La Narrativa del Fentanilo: Canadá y la Cifra del 1%

1. La Cifra Clave: Menos del 1% del Fentanilo Proviene de Canadá.

El argumento central y más repetido en las publicaciones es la estadística de que menos del 1% del fentanilo que ingresa a Estados Unidos proviene de Canadá. Los usuarios citan esta cifra, a menudo atribuyéndola a datos de la propia patrulla fronteriza estadounidense, como prueba irrefutable para contrarrestar la narrativa política.

  • Less than 1% of fentanyl coming into the u.s. Comes from canada. Same for illegal entry. Trump is a moron.
  • Facts don't matter to him. Less than 1% of the us fentanyl supply comes through canada. (0.2% to be precise). More comes up from the us than goes down. Much more.
  • Less than 1% of the fentanyl that enters the united states comes from canada, 97% comes from china by way of mexico.
  • Documented fact: less than 1% of fentanyl that goes into the us comes from canada. Far more crosses in the other direction.
  • Federal data shows less than 1% of fentanyl is smuggled in from canada.

2. Acusaciones Directas a Trump: Mentiras y Propaganda.

Una gran parte de los comentarios identifica a Donald Trump como el principal promotor de la desinformación. Se le acusa directamente de mentir, manipular y crear una crisis inexistente con fines políticos, utilizando un lenguaje duro y despectivo para calificar sus declaraciones y su persona.

  • Less than 1% of fentanyl comes into the u.s. From canada. Trump lies about everything & propagandizes everything.
  • Less than 1% of fentanyl enters the us from canada. Felon trump is lying!
  • You idiot. Less than 1% of fentanyl crosses the canadian border. Do you believe everything trump tells you?
  • Trump is a deceitful, abusive felon, rapist, and bully.
  • Stop reposting these without correcting it. He is bold faced lying. Less than 1% of fentanyl in the us is from canada.

3. Los Aranceles como Verdadero Motivo.

Los usuarios conectan la narrativa del fentanilo directamente con la imposición de aranceles a Canadá. La percepción general es que el problema de las drogas es una excusa o un pretexto para justificar medidas económicas proteccionistas que, de otro modo, serían ilegales o injustificadas bajo los acuerdos comerciales existentes.

  • Less than 1% of fentanyl entering the u.s. Comes from canada. Why is it not the u.s. Border patrol agency’s responsibility to stop it from entering? trump is lying to impose illegal tariffs.
  • The fentanyl bs is just a devious way for trump to circumvent the restrictions of the free trade agreement, which disallows these tariffs.
  • Trump is lying so he has an excuse to impose tariffs.
  • It is clear that since less than 1% of fentanyl crosses canada's border, that that is bs. Real reason for tariffs is to fund forthcoming tax cuts for the billionaires and corporations that bought trump the pesidency.
  • It was never about fentanyl. It was all about trump being an asshole.

Como contraargumento, muchos comentarios señalan que el verdadero problema de seguridad fronteriza es el flujo de armas ilegales y drogas desde Estados Unidos hacia Canadá, que consideran mucho más significativo que el tráfico inverso de fentanilo. Se acusa a EE. UU. de ignorar su propio papel en la desestabilización de la seguridad de su vecino.

  • Truth: less than 1% of fentanyl crosses border from canada into the us. Trump: drugs "pouring" into the us. Take care of your own house. And stop your lethal guns from "pouring" into canada.
  • Fact….less than 1% of fentanyl going to the us comes thru canada……1 fucking %! on the other hand 100’s of illegal guns cross the border into canada from the us!
  • Less than 1% of fentanyl comes from canada. The amount of illegal guns and drugs that come in from the usa however is substantially higher.
  • More fentanyl actually enters canada from the us, than the other way. Trump is just looking for any excuse.
