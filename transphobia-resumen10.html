10. Éxodo Digital: Usuarios Abandonan Twitter por la Transfobia

1. Twitter como Foco Principal de Transfobia.

Los usuarios describen de forma recurrente a Twitter como un "pozo ciego" o "pantano" lleno de odio, donde la transfobia es omnipresente y a menudo lo primero que encuentran al abrir la aplicación. Esta percepción es el principal catalizador para abandonar la plataforma.

  • I see stuff like this on twitter daily i think it's time i delete that cesspool of transphobia and hate.
  • Twitter is already a hate filled swamp of transphobia and racism.
  • Literally opened twitter and the first thing i came across was transphobia, i really need to ditch that app full time.
  • I open twitter in the first time in ages and it was just filled with transphobia and hate.
  • Yeah, i tried to look at twitter again but like, every single post is either quote-tweeting transphobia, or an innocuous post where the first several replies are transphobia. It's exhausting just to look at.

2. El Impacto en la Salud Mental.

La exposición constante a la transfobia tiene un grave impacto en el bienestar de los usuarios. Los testimonios describen la experiencia como agotadora, deprimente y perjudicial para la salud mental, hasta el punto de considerar el uso de la plataforma como una forma de autolesión.

  • It’s mainly the transphobia that’s finally pushed me over the edge with that platform. I can only take so much of constantly seeing people that want me dead on my timeline and in my replies.
  • Twitter is a form of self harm i swear i open the app and am immediately lambasted by transphobia.
  • I finally quit twitter when i realized how much the transphobia was fucking with my head. For some reason i had been assuming i was immune to such things lol.
  • I’m so exhausted by the constant transphobia that exists online. There are creators, who happen to be trans, not even talking about trans issues that get relentlessly harassed by bigots. People fucking suck.
  • That's a big part of why i no longer follow anyone who puts transphobia on my tl, even to dunk on it. It's far too corrosive to your mental health to have constant reminders of that shit.

3. La Búsqueda de Espacios Alternativos.

Ante el ambiente hostil, los usuarios migran activamente a otras redes sociales que perciben como más seguras o mejor moderadas. Expresan alivio al encontrar comunidades donde la transfobia no es tolerada y pueden interactuar de manera más positiva.

  • The transphobia is on my twitter feed is actually getting to such a bad level i might just delete it and start vibing on here instead.
  • I came here to escape the ridiculous amount of transphobia and hate that is now on twitter.
  • Transphobia on this app is just pitiful. Keep that shit on twitter. We moved for a reason!
  • This is the only platform where i feel safe (aside from a well-moderated mastodon instance), because actually comes down on transphobia at least on a community level.
  • Holy fucking shit there's so much less transphobia and nazism here than on fb and twitter. I thought ppl were exaggerating.

4. La Transfobia en Otras Plataformas.

Aunque Twitter es el principal foco de las quejas, los usuarios señalan que la transfobia es un problema generalizado en otras grandes plataformas como Facebook, Instagram, Threads y Reddit. La falta de moderación efectiva y los algoritmos que promueven contenido de odio son críticas comunes.

  • Tbh, i left facebook because the algo kept shoving aggressive transphobia in my face every 2 minutes and it was draining.
  • I experienced more transphobia and harassment on threads than i ever did on twitter. That place is a fucking nightmare.
  • The transphobia on twitter is exhausting and soul sucking but just as bad is the transphobia on facebook.
  • Unfortunately, reddit has a cesspool of transphobia all around; some queer subreddits are safe though. Not as bad as twitter, but it is still pretty bad.
  • I hate that transphobia isnt reportable on most large platforms anymore because all the tech oligarchs decided its cool actually.

5. La Naturaleza Obsesiva del Odio.

Varios usuarios observan un patrón de comportamiento obsesivo entre los acosadores, quienes parecen dedicar toda su identidad en línea a difundir transfobia. Se destaca cómo buscan activamente publicaciones sobre el tema para atacarlas, convirtiendo el odio en su única actividad.

  • My favorite thing about twitter is i can make some post about transphobia that gets 16 likes and somehow bigots will still find it and be in my replies. Are these people like… searching the word “transphobia” to make themselves angry?
  • This is especially funny because everone who gets into online transphobia posts about literally nothing else ever again. It completely consumes them.
  • Getting heaps of transphobic people on twitter and it's so funny how when you click on their accounts you find them overflowing with transphobia. Like no one is just a normal person with a lil transphobia on the side, it's an insane all consuming obsession that makes up their entire online identity.
  • Transphobes have no hobbies other than harassing us. They live on another planet istg.
  • Transphobia is a special kind of obsession.
