29. Bluesky: Un Refugio Digital Contra la Homofobia y el Odio

1. La Percepción de Bluesky como un Espacio Seguro.

Una gran cantidad de usuarios describe su experiencia en Bluesky como un alivio en comparación con otras plataformas. Celebran la ausencia de homofobia, racismo y misoginia, atribuyéndolo a una comunidad de personas que activamente huyeron de la toxicidad de redes como Twitter.

  • I love bluesky. It's the only social media app where i don't see racism, homophobia, transphobia, misogyny, ableism, etc. Tik tok, reddit, and twitter i see it everywhere.
  • Twitter - full of nazis. Actual nazis. Then there’s the homophobia, transphobic, the death threats, the lies. Bluesky is not like twitter. It’s people fed up of the hate who migrated here.
  • I'm fine in a bubble without racism, nazis, homophobia, and death threats. Been on bluesky for a while now and can't comprehend why people accepted that as the new normal on x.
  • Opens twitter; homophobia/transphobia, racism, conspiracy theories, and lots of other horrible stuff. Opens bluesky; people talking about their day, their interests, cool art, and people just having a good time. This app is such a breath of fresh air, have yet to see anything like that here.
  • The difference between bluesky and any other social media is so nice. There's not some idiot, commented negatively on every post. I don't come across homophobia at all. Instead i see empathetic, creative, intelligent people with something interesting to say. I hope it stays this way.

2. Moderación y el Debate sobre la "Cámara de Eco".

Los usuarios discuten activamente sobre las políticas de moderación de Bluesky. Mientras muchos las defienden como una herramienta necesaria para mantener un ambiente civilizado y reportan activamente el contenido de odio, otros critican su supuesta ineficacia. La acusación de ser una "cámara de eco" es rebatida como una preferencia por un espacio libre de intolerancia.

  • While bluesky is all to freedom of speech, it also has strict policies against harassment and hate, unlike twitter. Instead, to report all homophobia, racism & misogyny you see here.
  • I submitted numerous reports about homophobia on bluesky (including being doxxed, deadnamed, intimidated, and having my life threatened). Bluesky moderation did nothing.
  • Bluesky is a “liberal echo chamber”? if you mean that it’s a place where pernicious racism, homophobia & lies aren’t tolerated, then sure, i’ll take this echo chamber over the nazi nest of x.
  • I feel like the main reason a lot of far right people are instantly banned when they got on bluesky is because twitter doesn't really have any rules vs homophobia or transphobia but bluesky does.
  • So much racism, homophobia, ableism, transphobia reported, and not one bit of feedback from bluesky. Not one. This place is a joke.

3. La Infiltración del Odio y la Decepción.

A pesar del optimismo general, algunos usuarios expresan su preocupación y decepción al observar que la homofobia y otros comportamientos tóxicos están comenzando a aparecer en Bluesky. Esto se atribuye a la migración de "trolls" desde otras plataformas, lo que genera dudas sobre si Bluesky podrá mantener su promesa de ser un entorno más seguro.

  • Sadly, the increasing # of hateful posts means that the twitter/x trolls & assholes are migrating to bluesky. They got lonely unable to find people to direct their hate-filled toxic barbs toward & push their agenda of homophobia, misogyny & anti-blackness.
  • Oh lovely, just a few scrolls down and yet another bit of snappy homophobia. I come to bluesky to be humiliated, i guess.
  • I thought bluesky was supposed to be a safer environment than x and threads. I have not found this to be the case at all. I’ve blocked so many trigger words but i’m still seeing trolling. 2 much politics. Antisemitism. Homophobia. Hate speech.
  • Can someone tell me. If i get attacked here with homophobia then how is bluesky better than x.
  • As for me, the last time i had this "i was a philosophy major, leave my casual homophobia alone" type of exchange was on twitter. Seems like the unpleasant ppl have found bluesky. That's unfortunate.
